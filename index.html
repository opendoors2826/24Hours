Not Just a Party, A Sonic Devotion.

Experience the best of Chicago house and dance in solidarity with a living legend! More than a party, this 24-hour celebration will rally spiritual and material support for house music icon Peven Everett as he battles lung cancer.

✷ Live and virtual cameos from artists inspired by Peven

✷ Chicago-style food pop-ups to keep you nourished and dancing

✷ A rare, intimate live set from Peven Everett himself

Don't miss this once-in-a-lifetime chance to experience Peven's magic!

PEVEN
EVERETT

THE VOICE OF HOUSE MUSIC

Peven Everett's influence on house music runs deep. Known for his electrifying voice, multi-instrumental talent, and timeless anthems like "Stuck" and "Gabriel" he's shaped the sound of soulful house and beyond—collaborating with other legends like Roy Davis Jr. and Theo Parrish, and bridging gospel, jazz, funk, and dance.

This celebration honors not just his musical legacy, but his resilient spirit in his current battle with lung cancer.
