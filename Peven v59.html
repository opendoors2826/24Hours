Join us for an unforgettable 24-hour celebration honoring the legendary Peven Everett! From August 1st at 9pm to August 2nd at 9pm, we're transforming our Bay Area studio into a round-the-clock dance party—featuring live and virtual DJ sets, surprise cameos, Chicago-inspired food pop-ups and an electrifying sunset performance by Peven himself.

Every moment of this event supports Peven's battle against lung cancer, with 100% of donations going directly to his GoFundMe.

Don't miss this once-in-a-lifetime chance to experience Peven's magic!

THE VOICE OF HOUSE MUSIC

Peven Everett stands as one of house music's most distinctive voices, bringing soulful depth and Chicago authenticity to every performance. His journey through music has been one of constant evolution.

This celebration honors not just his musical legacy, but his resilient spirit in his current battle with lung cancer.
